At first, I will shortly introduce the routine-basis experiments and what we did.  Then Cindy will talk about lessons learned from the experiments.  In the routine-basis experiments, we daily executed a grid application that is TDDFT.  I will not talk about TDDFT here, but the execution model is a master-slave parallel model.  So, server calculates a main part.  When we implement TDDFT with Ninf-G2, a main part is divided into several independent tasks.  We ran this application many times using totally 10 sites during July 1 to August 31.  As a result, we figured out our testbed status clearly and what  should we discuss on this workshop.  We will talk about some difficulty of our testbed but it would be solved after the workshop, I hope.
